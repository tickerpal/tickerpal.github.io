About

A slackbot that allows you to get stock prices on demand in Slack.

Delivered with a simple Command

You can retrieve the the price of any stock by using the slash command /ticker followed by the stock ticker you are interested in.

Fully Customizable

Beyond the latest price for the stock price of the day you can display a chart, and some additional daily metrics.

Sharable Insights

Once you have the insights formed to your liking you can share them with your team as you see them.

Features

A few of the things that you can do with Tickerpal.

  • Simple Stock Quotes

    You can retrieve the the price of any stock by using the slash command /ticker followed by the stock ticker you are interested in.

  • Daily Chart

    In addition to the current price of the stock you are interested for the day there is a daily chart that tracks the price throughout the day.

  • In Depth Metrics

    In addition to the current price of a stock, with Tickerpal you have the ability to see the opening price, the volume of trades, the high, and low prices for the day.

  • Share with Your Channel

    Just by clicking the share button once you have the insights formed to your liking you can share them with your team as you see them.
